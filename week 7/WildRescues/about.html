About Us

Wild Rescues is a registered nonprofit wildlife rescue and rehabilitation facility in Ocala, Florida. We help injured, sick, neglected, and orphaned wildlife, rehabilitate them, and release them when possible.

We are members of the National Wildlife Rehabilitators Association.





We help many animals, such as:

  • Raccoons
  • Squirrels
  • Fox
  • Birds
  • Horses
  • Deer
  • Pigs
  • Reptiles


Did you find an injured or orphaned animal? Contact us to see if we can help.
